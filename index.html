Welcome to InterConnectNBCC

Welcome to InterConnectNBCC, the ultimate platform for international students studying technology at New Brunswick Community College! Our mission is to provide you with the tools, connections, and experiences you need to thrive in the tech industry. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that international students face, and we're committed to helping you overcome obstacles and achieve your goals. At InterConnectNBCC, you'll have the chance to connect with like-minded peers, engage with industry leaders, and explore exciting career prospects. So join us and discover what the future of tech has in store for you!

Join Us for Our Next Networking Event!

Join the Science Fair!

Attention all international students! Are you interested in participating in the annual Science Fair at NBCC? This is a great opportunity to showcase your scientific skills and knowledge, and meet other students from around the world. You can choose to work individually or in teams, and our faculty and staff are here to help you with any questions or support you may need. There will be prizes for the best projects, and we encourage all international students to join us and be a part of this exciting event!
